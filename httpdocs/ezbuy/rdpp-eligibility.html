Residential Daily Parking Permit Eligiblity

In order to purchase Residential Daily Parking Permits, you must meet the following criteria:

  1. You must live in a Residential Parking Zone.

  2. If you have bought Residential Daily Parking Permits within the past 30 days (either online or in person) then you are not eligibile to purchase additional permits at this time.

  3. You must purchase Residential Daily Parking Permits in person for your current zone at least once before you are eligible to purchase Residential Daily Parking Permits online.

    We require that you purchase Residential Daily Parking Permits in person in order to attain proof of address from a Driver's License or valid State ID.

    Only one in-person purchase is required to be eligible for future online transactions.
