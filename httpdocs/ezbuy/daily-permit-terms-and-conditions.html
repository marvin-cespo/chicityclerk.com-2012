  5. The Office of the City Clerk reserves the right to void any order – and void shipment of the order – if it is determined a purchase was made fraudulently.
  6. Once an order is placed by a purchaser, IT CANNOT BE CANCELLED. If a mistake is made, the purchaser will have to apply for a refund. The refund process can take up to 90 days.
