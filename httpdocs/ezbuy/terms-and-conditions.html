City Vehicle Sticker/Residential Permit Parking Daily Passes:

• If you have your vehicle sticker renewal application, enter your application number for a faster purchase.

• If you do not have your renewal number, you will need your driver's license number, license plate number and vehicle make/model to complete the transaction.

• If you are a senior citizen, the only way you can purchase your sticker online with Senior discount ($30) is with a renewal form that contains your renewal application number.

• If you do not have a senior renewal application form or application number and want to purchase the senior citizen discount sticker ($30), you must purchase your sticker in person or through the mail and provide documentation (e.g., valid IL driver's license) that proves you are 65 years of age or older.

• If you are attempting to purchase Daily Residential Parking Permits for the first time in the 2012-2013 city sticker renewal year and this purchase is not in conjunction with a city vehicle sticker purchase, you must visit a City Clerk facility and provide proof of residency to obtain your passes.

Payment Options:

American Express | Master Card | Visa | Discover Card

New Vehicle Purchase or New Resident or Prorated Fees:

• You have thirty (30) days from the date you moved to Chicago or from the date you purchased your vehicle to buy a City Vehicle Sticker without incurring a $60 late fee ($30 late fee for senior citizen stickers - Chicago Municipal Code 3-56-050(b)). All online transactions between July 16th and April 22nd will incur the late fee.

• In addition, new residents and new vehicle purchases are eligible to purchase a vehicle sticker at a prorated fee.
